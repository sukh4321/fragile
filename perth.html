Stress Free Moving In Perth

Planning to move your home or office somewhere in Perth? It can be a challenging DIY project if you’re a very busy person and you’ve got no experience with moving. You could probably do it but it will require time and effort … and by the time you’re finished, you will probably be stressed out.

Fragile’s professional movers in Perth are here to help you make your move stress-free. It’s been our bread and butter to help make relocation easy for individuals and businesses for the past 15 years. If you’re still on the fence about hiring professional movers, here are some questions and answers that might help you make a decision.

How can your company help me with packing?

Fragile Removals can provide packing materials like sturdy boxes, crates and bubble wrap to help you pack your items. If you avail of our packing services, we can send trained packers who know the different packing techniques necessary to protect your items so that the move will have zero damaged items. The number of packers will depend on how much stuff you have and how much time there is to finish the packing.

Packing is done systematically, that is, items are packed room by room and boxes or crates are properly labeled to make sure that unpacking will be easy and organized. Our packers can also help with the unpacking and putting of things in their proper place. You only need to give clear instructions regarding what goes where.

I need to move big items, can you help me?

Moving big items, such as furniture, instruments or sports equipment, needs extra thought and care. In some cases, it may be necessary to get the assistance of a handyman to dismantle a big cabinet, for example, before transporting it. In other cases, there’s no other choice but to carry the big, heavy item, like a couch or table, in one piece. You would need people with skills and experience to move these types of items. Our professional movers in Perth can do this for you.

Regarding transportation, Fragile Removals has different sizes of trucks and we will have a truck that is just the right size to transport your big items. Our movers understand how to properly secure your items in place and allot enough space in between so that they will not be damaged while in transit.

Upon reaching the destination, you can also depend on our team of movers to carry your items inside your new home or office being careful not to damage them or the floor, walls or doors of your property. Our movers may also reassemble your furniture, if necessary.

Are your movers employees or contractors?

When you hire removalists in the Perth area, you want to know how trustworthy the movers on the crew are. We can assure you that we follow the standards set by the Australian Furniture Remover Association or AFRA. The movers we send to our clients from Perth and from all our offices Australia-wide are trained employees of the company who know the best practices of the moving industry.

We have done background checks on each of our employees as our business highly values the trust that our clients give us. We don’t hire people that would jeopardize that trust. That is also the reason why we don’t hire contractors or third parties to do the job. There have been many cases that the contracted parties do not show up on moving day or just don’t do a good enough job, thereby ruining the good reputation of the moving company. All our employees are reliable and good honest workers with all the necessary qualifications, training and background checks.

How much do I need to pay for your services?

The amount that you will pay for our Perth removalists services will depend on a number of factors. The volume of items to transfer, the distance between the two locations, the day when moving is scheduled and the add-on services availed – all these factors need to be considered when counting the cost.

You can request a free quote over the phone or via the form on our official webpage to get an estimate of moving costs. One of our professional movers can also visit your home or office in Perth to get a more detailed understanding of your relocation needs, which allows us to provide you with a more accurate quote.

So contact our Perth office today or fill out the quick quote form near the top of this page and we will get back to you within 10 minutes with a moving quote (Monday – Friday 9.00 am to 5.00 pm)

We look forward to helping make your move a lot easier – and taking the stress out of your big move.
