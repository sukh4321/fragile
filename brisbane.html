You Can Rely On Fragile Removals Brisbane

If you’re confronted with the challenging task of relocating your home or office to Brisbane, Fragile Removals is your go to moving company. Besides the professional service and competitive pricing offered to you, here are some of the reasons why hiring us can help you have the most productive and hassle-free moving experience.

Office removals with minimal business interruption

Business owners need to consider the disruption in operations before moving forward with office relocation. If everyone is busy doing their business as usual, it would be difficult to take care of the tasks involved with relocating. Fragile Removals can take care of the tasks associated with the move, such as the packing and removal of office supplies, furniture and equipment.

f there are sensitive documents that need special handling, we’ll make sure that only authorized people handle them. We can work during off-peak hours so that our diligent movers can do their job with less disruption to your business operations. Just let us know your preferred schedule so we can arrange the best time for the move.

Efficient house moves thanks to add-on services

Whether you’re relocating within Brisbane or interstate, there are a lot of things to think about. There are things to pack, furniture and appliances to prep for the move, utilities to manage, small repair jobs to take care of and clutter to clean up. Doing all these things may just be too much for some people who have other more important things to do. Our team of movers and service providers can help perform all the above necessary tasks efficiently. To let us know which services are to be included in your requested quote, simply talk to one of our customer service agents.

Packing services

Fragile Removals Brisbane is an AFRA-accredited moving company. This means that we have satisfied the requirements for equipment, transportation and trained personnel to complete a move without a hitch. Our packers are fully knowledgeable about the proper packing of different items, including fragile objects, high valued items like paintings or antiques and things with irregular shape and size that don’t fit in a box.

Furniture removal

Whether there’s a need to dismantle large items of furniture or move heavy stuff like a dining table or home gym equipment, our staff have the necessary tools to facilitate such tasks. It can be quite tricky to move these big and bulky items. Without proper techniques, the item itself and even the wall, door or floor of your property might be damaged. But our movers know exactly what to do to load these into our trucks and transport them to the new location while keeping them damage-free. We are happy to say that we have served countless clients who had absolute peace of mind because of the premium quality moving service we provided.

Handyman services

House and office moves usually involve small repair jobs, be it in the place you just left or in the one you’re moving into. Some common small jobs that can be done by a handyman include carpet removal, replacements of broken flooring, chipped or cracked tiles and dismantling of furniture, such as built-in cabinets. When you occupy your new place, you might also find the need for small electrical repairs, such as reconnecting utilities, fixing busted lighting or broken electrical outlets. If you need an expert handyman in Brisbane, we can put you in touch with the most reliable local handyman services.

Cleaning services

If you don’t want to leave behind a messy place, which results from having packed up all the stuff you want to bring with you, but you’re too tired to do it yourself, then you need to get cleaning services. If you don’t want to see clutter in your new place after unpacking, but you’re too occupied reorganizing and putting things where they belong, then you need cleaning services. You can request cleaners that come highly recommended by Fragile Removals Brisbane.

Temporary Storage Facilities with Insurance

A lot of unexpected things could happen while moving. You might discover that you want to move more things than your new space could accommodate. Or perhaps your sofa won’t fit through the doorway. What is really disappointing is when your new house contractor doesn’t finish their work in time and your scheduled move is already due. You have no choice but to find a temporary holding space for all your stuff.

The good news is there is an affordable storage option offered by Fragile Removals. We have different sizes of storage space available to suit the volume of items you need to hold for short or long term. This Brisbane storage facility is secure and well-maintained. We also have insurance policies to cover the goods entrusted to us by our clients.

Moreover, we have a fleet of trucks to facilitate door-to-door delivery of our customers’ belongings if that is the requested service of our customer.

No hidden fees – What We Quote is What You Pay

When we give our prospective customers a free quote, we do our best to give an amount that is essentially what they need to pay. Nobody wants to see hidden fees included in the final billing, right? That is why, we prefer to have our customer service agent do an in-house visit to see how much stuff a client wants to move and ask questions, such as what services he/she wants to include and the travel distance between the two locations. By understanding what services a client really wants, we can give the most accurate quote with no hidden extras.

It is also a pleasure to mention at this point that we are not like some moving companies in Brisbane that charge extra payment for fuel levies. We also don’t charge for the extra effort that our staff needs to undertake to move things through steep driveways or up a flight of stairs. We pride ourselves in being able to provide a quality of service that’s really worth what you pay for.

So if you are looking to move to or from Brisbane – give Fragile Removals a call at our Brisbane office – or else fill out the contact form near the top of the page and we will have our consultant get back to you within 10 minutes during normal business hours. We look forward to providing you the most affordable quote – and also know we will provide you with a fantastic and reliable service on moving day.
