Why Choose Us As Your Movers?

Fragile Removals is a trusted removalist company in Australia, with a track history of more than 15 years of providing premium and competitively priced services. There are many reasons why chose us for your relocation, including:

Reliable, Professional, Experienced

Our team of movers are professionally trained to meet the high standards set by the Australian Furniture Remover Association, they have all been police checked to insure our clients are in good hands. Our workers undergo Occupational Health and Safety training as part on our comprehensive training modules, that cover not only the physical aspects of furniture removal but extend to customer contact, customer service, company vision and corporate goals, insuring you get not only experienced professionals, but movers that understand our clients and the level of service we require in order to position our service above other industry standards. Industry Accredited

Fragile is proud to be a member of the Australian Furniture Removers Association (AFRA) that acts as a regulator for the industry and insures all registered moving companies conform to a strict code of conduct, hold all required insurance policies, provide training to all staff members, insures that offices and storage facilities are fit for public access and that appropriate vehicles are used to provide the service. Using an AFRA member insures your choice of mover anywhere in Australia is a sound choice!

How can I be sure that your people can do the job well?

Our company takes pride in providing an excellent service to our clients. But this would not be possible if we didn’t have the right people for the job. Our team of Melbourne movers have been through through strict qualification and training before being deployed to meet our clients face to face.

As an accredited member of AFRA (Australian Furniture Remover Association), we need to make sure that all our staff are professionally trained to follow (or better yet exceed) the set standards in the moving industry. Moreover, our movers are reliable, prompt, friendly, courteous and helpful in providing excellent customer service, which is attested to by many of our clients in the past 15 years.

How much does your moving service cost?

The cost of moving depends on many factors, such as the distance from the pick-up point in Melbourne to the planned destination, the quantity and type of possessions to be moved, how many movers will be needed and the time of the day or the day of the week when the move will take place. Generally, you can expect a higher price for moving a big quantity of items over a long distance scheduled to be transported on a Saturday or Sunday. The price may also be affected by the extra services that a client would avail for the entire process of the move. The best way to know a more or less accurate cost of your relocation needs is to get a free quote.

How secure are my belongings if I choose your moving company in Melbourne?

If you choose Fragile Removals to move the valuables of your home or office, we assure you that we will take the utmost care to protect everything from being broken or damaged. Our professional moving staff know how to carry things in and out of a place the right way, so that there will be no damage to any of your possessions and even to the floor, doors and walls of your new property.

But, in case an accident happens and things get broken (though this is very unlikely) there is no need to worry. We have insurance policies to cover the goods we transport. Just ask about the details of our insurance coverage from our customer support team, so you can be worry-free when moving with us. In any case, we give assistance to our clients should there be a need to claim damages.

Do you offer packing services? What other services do you offer?

If you’re very busy and need help packing your stuff, our expert packers in Melbourne can also help you with that. Packing of items is usually done per room so that unpacking them later in your new place will be easier and more organized. Our packing system uses a combination of packing supplies (cardboard boxes, bubble wraps, crates, straps, etc) to ensure that different items, i.e. clothes, fragile things, furniture and appliances, are packed accordingly so they are not broken or damaged when loading, unloading and transporting.

In case you also need temporary storage for your possessions, we also provide this service to many of our clients. We have secure storage facilities located in Melbourne, so whether you are moving in or moving out of the city, we can take care of storing your belongings while waiting for the new place to be ready. If you’re ready to get your stuff back, just let us know and we’ll deliver them right to your front door.

Can your transport vehicle accommodate all our stuff?

Fragile Removals has different sizes of trucks that can accommodate both small and big moving jobs to any area in Melbourne. We make sure to maximize the space in the truck while ensuring that everything is secure while in transit. By determining the truck size that is most appropriate to the volume of belongings that our clients have, our clients are able to save more on delivery costs. This is especially important when moving over long distances as it translates to more gas savings and shorter delivery time.
